Betta Fish

Also known as Siamese fighting fish, they come from Southeast Asia and are popular in aquariums worldwide.

Guppy Fish

Guppy fish are small, live-bearing tropical freshwater fish known for their vibrant colors and adaptability.

Molly Fish

Molly fish are freshwater fish with various species, often chosen for their peaceful nature and ease of care.

Gold Fish

Goldfish are the most popular pet fish, A pet goldfish can live for up to ten years, Pet Goldfish can recognize different human faces and voices

Platy Fish

Platies are easy to care for, they breed quickly, and their keeping and feeding regimens can be easily met even by a beginner aquarist.

Angelfish Fish

There are around 90 species of marine angelfish. Some species of angelfish can live up to 15 years.

Swordtail Fish

They are friendly, gentle fish that flourish in small groups and interact with other fish. They are live bearers, which means they give birth to live young instead of laying eggs

Danios Fish

Zebra danios have a silvery-gold coloration with blue striped markings running lengthwise from the gills to the tail. They grow up to two inches.

Neon tetra Fish

Neon tetras are shoaling fish that do best when kept in groups of six or more. Neon tetras change their coloration in response to the lighting of the environment.
